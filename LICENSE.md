## Why GPL-3.0-or-later

Same rationale as the framework: BoringOS is plumbing for AI agents — the value compounds when others can fork, extend, and ship their own modules. Copyleft keeps that loop honest. If you ship a fork or a derivative module, you ship the source.

For the framework's full licensing rationale, see [BoringOS-dev/boringos § docs/licensing.md](https://github.com/BoringOS-dev/boringos/blob/main/docs/licensing.md).
